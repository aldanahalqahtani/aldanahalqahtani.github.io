Growing the next generation of Saudi plant scientists.

Science grows through people. I mentor undergraduate students at Prince Sattam bin Abdulaziz University through hands-on research in plant genomics, transcriptomics, phytochemistry, and bioinformatics — equipping a new generation of Saudi scientists with the tools to bioprospect, conserve, and innovate.

Several of our student teams have been recognized in inter-university research competitions. Their work is showcased below.
