Research

My Work at a Glance

From molecules, to meaning, to impact.

My research traces plant secondary metabolites from their origin in DNA all the way to the molecules themselves — combining genomics, transcriptomics, metabolomics, and bioinformatics. The goal is to understand how Saudi plants build their natural chemistry, and to translate that chemistry into discoveries for human health, biotechnology, and environmental sustainability.
