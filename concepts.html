Concepts & Terms

Definitions and short explanations

Plant Biology

Secondary Metabolite

A natural molecule produced by a plant that is not directly involved in basic growth or reproduction, but that helps the plant survive challenges like predators, drought, or salt. Many of these compounds become medicines and natural products.

Multi-omics

Transcriptome

The complete set of messenger RNA (mRNA) molecules present in a cell or tissue at a given moment — showing which genes are actively switched on. Studying it reveals how a plant responds to its environment in real time.

Bioinformatics

Bioprospecting

The systematic search for valuable molecules in nature — particularly in plants, microbes, and marine organisms — that can be developed into medicines, foods, or sustainable industrial products.
